What is Dingocoin?

Dingocoin is a decentralized, peer-to-peer digital currency that enables you to easily send money online. Think of it as "the great Dingo internet currency."

Created in parody of Dogecoin for absolutely everyone.

The family fun internet currency?

Dingocoin sets itself apart from other digital currencies with an amazing, vibrant community made up of friendly folks just like you."

What's with Dingocoin and the dingo?

The "Dingo" is our fun, friendly mascot! The Dingo is a breed of dog that lives wild and represents Dingocoin.
